Don't trust anyone

Your data is safe. Nobody will be able to see it, not even us!

All the files you upload are encrypted and stored on multiple cloud providers (like Google Drive, One Drive, ...). Each provider stores an encrypted portion of the file, and that makes the information unreadable. Files will be encrypted on your side and that makes the data unreadable even for us.

Too many cloud services?

Connecting multiple cloud providers to HashDrive makes all their space available!

All the space available on the services you connect to HashDrive is available through us. You don't have to manage each one seperately, just connect it to HashDrive, make your files truly private and unify the space.
